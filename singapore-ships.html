Ships of the Singapore Strait

As an aviation enthusiast, I have always enjoyed planespotting ✈️. Recently, I was in Asia, in particular in the Singapore Strait, which is located between Singapore and Malaysia to the north and Indonesia's Riau Islands to the south.


There, I had the opportunity to do some shipspotting 🛳️. The Singapore Strait is one of the world's busiest commercial shipping corridors, so there was a wide variety of ships to be spotted. Hope you enjoy some of them below! 😀
