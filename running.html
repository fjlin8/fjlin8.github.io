Running

Running is one of my passions. I started running casually in high school before taking a break during college and the years following. After moving to NYC in 2021, I started running more and ran my first half marathon and marathon in early 2022. At the end of 2023, I decided to try to be more consistent with running to see where it would take me, which brings me to the present.

I've run four marathons since starting my running journey:

  • 2024-11 New York: 2:49:05
  • 2024-05 Geneva: 2:53:24
  • 2023-11 New York: 3:17:40
  • 2022-04 Paris: 3:22:51
