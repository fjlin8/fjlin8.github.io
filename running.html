Running

Running is one of the activities I enjoy. I started running casually in high school before taking a break during college and the years following. After moving to NYC in 2021, I started running more and ran my first half marathon and marathon in early 2022. At the end of 2023, I decided to try to be more consistent with running to see where it would take me, which brings me to the present.

I've ran three marathons:

  • 2022-04 Paris: 3:22:51
  • 2023-11 New York: 3:17:40
  • 2024-05 Geneva: 2:53:24
