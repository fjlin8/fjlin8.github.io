Cycling

Cycling is another one of my passions. Unlike running (where I have tried to improve my performance), I prefer to treat cycling as a leisurely activity. I started casually biking in 2019 and got more into long rides during 2020 before doing my first century ride in the summer of 2022. I have also gotten to explore some cool places while traveling through biking.

I've done five century rides since starting my cycling journey:

  • 2025-07-13 Montauk Ride (128.23 mi)
  • 2025-06-22 Schenectady-Poughkeepsie Ride (114.58 mi)
  • 2024-06-30 Brooklyn - Queens Century Ride (108.56 mi)
  • 2023-07-23 Tappan Zee Century Ride (100.68 mi)
  • 2022-07-30 Rockaways Century Ride (100.07 mi)
