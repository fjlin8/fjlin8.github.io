Podcast on My Life

Recently, my friend showed me NotebookLM, a tool developed by Google that uses Gemini to interact with document sources to generate summaries, analyses, explanations, and more.

I uploaded the pages on my personal website (the one you're reading right now) to NotebookLM to generate a podcast episode about my life, and played around with some prompt engineering to see what different outputs it could produce. I was impressed with the results, in particular its ability to make the discussion sound so natural and conversational with ad-libs!

I ended up liking the following audio clip the most and uploaded it to YouTube. Feel free to check it out if you're interested!
